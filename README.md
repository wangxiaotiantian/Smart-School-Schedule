# 🎒 Smart School Schedule Master | 智能课程表助手

[English] | [简体中文]

One-click to turn your school schedule image into a smart calendar and a beautiful printable chart.
只需一张课程表图片，秒变带提醒的智能日历和手绘风精美课表。

## 🌟 Features / 功能
- **AI OCR**: Auto-extract schedule from images. (AI 自动提取图片课表)
- **Smart Reminder**: Notification at 21:00 the night before class days. (前晚 9 点自动提醒准备书包)
- **Excalidraw Style**: Hand-drawn style printable layout. (手绘风精美课表)
- **Privacy First**: Runs locally on your AI tool. (本地运行，隐私安全)

## 🚀 How to Use / 如何使用
1. Download this repo and open it in **Cursor**, **Claude Code**, or **OpenClaw**.
   下载本项目并在 AI 编辑器中打开。
2. Drag your schedule image into the chat.
   将课表图片拖入对话框。
3. Type: *"Process this from 2026-02-15 to 2026-06-30."*
   输入：“处理这张课表，学期从 2026-02-15 到 2026-06-30。”
